Final project - restaurant orders

Authentication - JWT
Authorization - Seperate Roles with different authentization for customers and manager,
    fake users can be reported and blocked
Languages - Support 3 Languages, customer can make an order in one language and the restaurant can 
            get it in another language.
Socket.io - restaurant will get notifications about new orders.
Emails - Restaurant will get emails about new orders.
MoongoDB tables - Users, Roles (manager, customer), meals, additions, orders, calls (Bill, waiter) ....
Cache - Managing cached data (maybe redis),
Stock - meals with no ingridiants should not be displayed to the users.
Menu - Manager can add/reomve items to the menu, or update quantity
Manager can add users and grant them autherization to the restaurant page.
